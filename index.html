Crystal collector!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four chrystals below. By clicking on a chrystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game if your total score equals the random number. You will lose if your score exceeds the random number.
  • The value of each chrystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, both the target score and the value of the chrystal will be randomly generated.
